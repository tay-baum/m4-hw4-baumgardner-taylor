The Band

Judah & the Lion is an American Alternative and Folk band that came together while students at Belmont University. After winning Belmont's 2012 Best of the Best competition, they went on to release their first ep in June of the same year. After quickly charting on Billboard's Bluegrass Albums in 2013, they began opening for Drew Holcomb and the Neighbors in 2014. Their debute full length studio album also charted in 2014 and their careers snowballed from there. Almost 10 years later, they have released 4 albums, a number of singles, made many television appearances, and toured the US, Canada, and Europe.

Revival

releasing June 10th!

Judah & the Lion's Revivial comes at a moment of rejuvenation and evolution for the band, both personally and professionally, with a world looking to get back to normal. Judah Akers and Brian Macdonald return with the band's fourth album that aims to channel the energy and spirit presented at the beginning of the group's journey. Revival is a collection of songs that speaks to the band's return to innocence and hope through reclaimation. They look inwards on life's changes as a group that still finds energy in innocence after touring for 8 years and reaching heights on the Billboard charts and earning awards like iHeart Music's Best New Alternative Rock Artist of the Year in 2018.

Up to the realease of the new album, a number of singles have been released, the latest of which is "scream!"

JUDAH

In 2020, the band's lead vocalist and guitarist, Judah Akers, began releasing solo music under the artist name JUDAH.

JUDAH is the surgacing of a compelling and raw journey expressed through shouts of praise and tumultuous reflection. Not only does he have story to tell but has been gifted with an honest ability to connect with people through music. The hope for JUDAH is to scream out Christ's love to followers and doubters alike, and share the real grace and redemption that's been found by someone walking in their shoes.

JUDAH is unapologetically authentic and straighforward, ripping through the layers of stigma and discomfort. He comes with a fresh approach that reflects his personality and style of praise and worship through music.
